Paint Protection Films

Paint protection film (PPF) is a thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU) film that is used to coat the painted surfaces of new or old cars to help shield the paint from small abrasions, stone chips, and bug splatters.

Benefits of Paint Protection Films

Scratch Resistance

PPF provides a durable barrier against scratches, chips, and abrasions caused by road debris, rocks, gravel, and other environmental hazards. It helps preserve the original paint finish of the vehicle.

Invisible Protection

PPF is transparent and virtually invisible when properly installed, maintaining the original aesthetics of the vehicle without altering its appearance. It allows the beauty of the paint finish to shine through while providing essential protection.

Stain Resistance

PPF is resistant to stains from bird droppings, tree sap, insects, and other contaminants. Its smooth surface makes it easier to clean, reducing the need for frequent detailing and preserving the vehicle's appearance
